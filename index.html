Talia Moore

The Blend NYC

The Blend NYC is a section of a larger collaborative transmedia project called Identity NYC. This series will include the stories of individuals who identity as mixed racial and mixed ethnic in New York City. The Blend NYC will discuss their experiences with their identities, cultural differences within their blended heritage, and challenges they may face. The project will explore these stories through a digital platform, photography and and photo album of archival family photographs.
